Treatments for Humans


One in five Kiwis is living with chronic pain. Research tells us that majority of musculoskeletal pain conditions are related to structural misalignment. Bowen therapy may effectively resolve these type of structure-related chronic pain through whole-body-re-balancing. In addition, mobility problems from such as stroke, Parkinson’s disease or spinal injuries may be helped with Bowen through improved coordination of movement. Also those suffering from recurring ailments such as arthritis, migraine, sleeping disorders, digestive trouble, reproductive issues, and respiratory problems can be assisted with Bowen Therapy.

  • Pain (back, neck, cancer therapy, coccyx, sciatic, fibromyalgia, trigeminal neuralgia)
  • Joint problems (frozen shoulder, elbow/wrist injury, meniscus injury, temporomandibular joint)
  • Soft tissue injuries (Achilles tendon injury, hamstring tightness, tendonitis, sprain/strain)
  • Arthritis (osteoarthritis, rheumatoid, lupus)
  • Movement dysfunctions (Parkinson's disease, stroke, Cerebral Palsy, spinal injuries)
  • Neurologic disorders (migraine, autism, concussion, vertigo, trauma, chronic fatigue, epilepsy)
  • Reproductive disorders (PMS, hot flashes, pregnancy complications, endometriosis)
  • Digestive disorders (irritable bowel syndrome, baby colic, Crohn's disease)
  • Respiratory problems (breathing difficulties, asthma, spasmodic dysphonia, sinus congestion)
  • Rare and incurable diseases (quality of life improvement)
