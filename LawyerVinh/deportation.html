Utah Immigration Deportation Law

Deportation. It is a word that no one likes to hear. If you have been living in the United States – either on a temporary visa or even illegally – you know the value of making sure that you have a clean record. However, if you have been charged or convicted of a crime in this country, you need to be aware of state and federal guidelines relating to your status. But perhaps the most important consideration is your choice of attorney to assist you in preventing the threat of deportation.

Salt Lake City, Utah, lawyer Vinh K. Ly brings to his practice the skill, understanding, and knowledge you need. Contact an immigration deportation law attorney today for more information.

My insight into your deportation case comes from my own experience coming to this country as a refugee from Vietnam. I understand the concerns and situations that arise when you are learning to acclimate to a different culture.

If you have been charged with a crime and fear deportation, I can look at your situation and determine quickly if the crime constitutes grounds for deportation in the state of Utah. I also assist clients with the following matters related to deportation:

  • Habeas Petitions
  • Voluntary Departures
  • Expediting Deportation Cases
  • Domestic Violence Cases
  • Drug or Weapons Possession
  • Convictions
  • Appeals
  • Sex
  • Drug Crimes

Has your deportation case been held up or lost in the system? I am not afraid of suing the government in order to expedite the situation. In court, I can help better your chances of being able to remain in this country through plea-bargaining, calling for mistrial, or researching your charge or conviction and determining if the terminology accurately reflects the situation. You need not be afraid of working to maintain your status within this country.
