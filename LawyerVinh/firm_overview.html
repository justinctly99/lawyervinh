Firm Overview

Salt Lake City Immigration, Personal Injury, and Criminal Defense Law Firm

At the Law Office of Vinh K. Ly, Attorney & Counselor at Law, no immigration case is too small. I work with clients from all walks of life—students, scholars, specialized workers, business owners, investors, asylees, religious workers, and individuals with extraordinary abilities in business, science, education, athletics, or arts—providing clear, confident, and positive advice and legal service related to their immigration law situations.

No matter how small the case, I can help!

When I was a child, I came to this country from Vietnam as a refugee. I now use this background as well as my legal experience to make the process of living, working, running a business, or studying in the United States simpler for people from other countries. Contact an immigration law attorney you can trust – make an appointment to speak with me today.

Whether you are seeking assistance from an attorney well-versed in deportation law, or wanting to appeal through the courts a verdict that may negatively affect your status in this country, a lawyer can provide you with advice and representation that is as positive as it is effective. I primarily work with clients seeking legal counsel for matters involving:

  • Visas
  • Green cards
  • deportation
  • Criminal defense related to immigration problems
  • Personal Injury

I have worked with clients from diverse backgrounds and from different countries throughout South America, Central America, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia, providing the same accessible, friendly service to each client. Much of the work I do comes from both repeat client business and referrals from countless satisfied individuals now successfully living and working in the United States.
